Executive Summary

A cross-platform mention scan was conducted for "Macquarie University" across YouTube, TikTok, and the Facebook Ad Library in the Thailand market. Of the 16 companies on Macquarie's official Thailand agent register, only two — Education For Life and AECC Global Thailand — have produced identifiable promotional content. The remaining 14 authorised agents, including One Education Consulting, show zero mention activity across all scanned platforms.

Education For Life is the clear promotion leader with 4 dedicated YouTube videos and 4,924 combined views. One critical compliance issue was identified: Unicentre College Thailand is actively promoting Macquarie University on YouTube despite not appearing on the official agent register. This warrants immediate review by Macquarie's partnership team.

TikTok represents a completely untapped channel — no Thai education agent has produced Macquarie-branded content on TikTok, despite the platform's dominance among Thai student audiences aged 18–24.

Compliance Alert — Unauthorised Promotion

⚠️

Unicentre College Thailand — Not on Official Macquarie Agent Register

Unicentre College Thailand operates a YouTube channel and has uploaded at least 2 Macquarie University promotional videos ("Macquarie — Shape the future of business" and "Macquarie — Join our collective", both dated November 2019). This company does not appear anywhere on the official Macquarie Thailand agent register scraped on 13 April 2026. Either their authorisation has lapsed without content being removed, or they are promoting Macquarie without authorisation. Macquarie's International Partnerships team should verify and, if necessary, request removal or issue an updated register listing.

Key Findings

5 findings identified
Finding 1 — Promotion Leader
Education For Life is Macquarie's most active Thailand promoter with 4 dedicated YouTube videos spanning 2019–2024 and 4,924 combined views. Their Thai-language campus tour series ("พาทัวร์") is the only Macquarie content in Thai on any platform. They hold a significant organic advantage.
Finding 2 — Quiet Authorised Agent
AECC Global Thailand holds an authorisation and has produced 2 YouTube videos (2018 and 2021), but both are outdated with a combined 77 views. No activity has been detected in the last 4 years. Their Macquarie promotion is effectively dormant.
Finding 3 — One Education Gap
One Education Consulting is an authorised Macquarie agent but has zero promotional content visible on YouTube, TikTok, or Facebook. There is no organic or paid mention of Macquarie University attributable to One Education on any scanned platform.
Finding 4 — TikTok is Open
No Thai education agent is running Macquarie content on TikTok. This platform reaches the exact demographic Macquarie targets (Thai undergraduates aged 18–24) and is completely uncontested. The first agent to claim this space sets the benchmark.
Finding 5 — Unauthorised Promotion
Unicentre College Thailand published 2 Macquarie promotional videos in November 2019 and is not listed on the current official agent register. This is either a lapsed authorisation with content not removed or active promotion without approval — both require follow-up.
🎯 Top 3 Recommended Actions — Macquarie Partnership Team
1
URGENT
Investigate and resolve the Unicentre College Thailand compliance issue
Unicentre College Thailand has published Macquarie-branded promotional content on YouTube but does not appear on the current official Thailand agent register. Macquarie's International Partnerships team should verify whether this company held a prior authorisation (and when it lapsed), contact Unicentre to request removal or reinstatement, and confirm no other unregistered agents are promoting the university across the Thailand market.
Evidence: 2 YouTube videos, Nov 2019, still publicly visible
2
HIGH
Brief all 16 authorised Thailand agents on content expectations and TikTok opportunity
Only 2 of 16 authorised agents have ever produced Macquarie content. A simple agent activation brief — including suggested content formats (campus tours in Thai, program spotlights, student testimonials) and platform priorities (TikTok first, then YouTube) — could significantly expand Macquarie's organic reach in Thailand at no cost to the university. Education For Life's Thai-language campus tour videos should be shared as the benchmark format for other agents to replicate.
Opportunity: 14 authorised agents with zero Macquarie content · TikTok completely uncontested
3
MEDIUM
Enable Facebook Ad Library monitoring by connecting an access token
Paid advertising data for Thailand was not retrievable in this scan due to API authentication requirements. Facebook ads are a primary student recruitment channel for several Bangkok-based agencies. To complete the picture, Macquarie's digital team or a nominated Thailand agent should provide a Facebook user access token (with ads_read permission) to allow automated weekly monitoring of paid Macquarie promotions across the Thailand market. This will reveal which agents are investing media spend on Macquarie and at what scale.
